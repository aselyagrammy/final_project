Career Overview

Total films: 24 (1993 – 2024)

Bayan Maksatovna Alaguzova (maiden name: Mukhitdenova; formerly Yesentaeva) is a Kazakhstani producer, TV presenter, film actress, and singer.

She gained her first fame in 1993 thanks to her leading role in the melodrama "Mahabbat beketi" (Station of Love). Following this, she worked as a journalist and presenter on television for many years.

Since 2006, she has been producing Kazakhstani musical groups, including the well-known "KeshYou", as well as individual performers such as Zhanar Dugalova and Yerke Esmakhan. Bayan has also appeared in several films and TV series.

Current Role

Bayan is the head of the Shine Production company. She is currently married to Tursengali Alaguzov.
